Welcome

Do you aspire to follow in the hoof prints of Olympic gold medal winning showjumpers? Or just enjoy a pony ride with friends in a fun and safe environment? Look no further, you've just found the perfect riding school right here in the North East


The Bowes Manor Equestrian Centre has been helping children achieve their dreams, develop skills, and become part of a team for over 40 years. As a Certified Equine Association (CEA) accredited facility, the Bowes Manor team cater for riders having their first lead rein lessons, all the way through to winning at the Horse of the Year Show. We also offer pony parties, day camps,on-site gymkhanas and show jumping events.

First class livery facilities are available on full, part, or DIY, with access to indoor and outdoor riding arenas. We provide a full set of showjumps, competition training, and schooling for both horses and riders. We host regular events, and there is also an onsite cafe.

The first steps toward fulfilling your equestrian potential start here.
