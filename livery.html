Livery

We offer DIY, full and part livery service, we have staff on hand who are able to take care of your horse and ponies needs on a daily basis at a small additional cost. We can ride, muck-out and clip if requested. We also have 45 acres of grass available for turnout which can be used all year round.

We have an indoor arena and two outdoor arenas which are available for use throughout the day and evening. We also have turnout space on the yard that can be used daily too. ​

Within the cost of your weekly livery, hay/haylage is provided and the use of all facilities offered.

Please contact us via 01914109703 for more details on livery at Bowes Manor Equestrian Centre.
