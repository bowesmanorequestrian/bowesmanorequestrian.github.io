Lessons

At Bowes Manor Equestrian Centre we offer professional training to all levels and ages of rider, whether you're looking to build your skills in a group lesson or work at your own pace in a private lesson! Take a look at the different types of lessons we offer below

Group Lessons

In these lessons you will receive a 45 minutes of instruction in either our Olympic size all weather arena, or in our large indoor arena depending on the weather (none of our lessons are cancelled whatever the weather) you will then have a fifteen minute hack along the bridle ways that surround our equestrian centre.

Private Lessons

We also provide 45 minute private lessons

Pony Parties

Stuck for an idea for your childs birthday? Why not suprise them with a pony party? All of your guests will recieve a 45 minute lesson which will include fun games for them to play whilst on their ponies, and then a fifteen minute hack around the bridle ways that surround our equestrian centre with beautiful scenery, then feed their ponies a little treat. After their ride, you can enjoy our function room where you can either bring along some food and a birthday cake or it can be arranged through the centre at an additional cost, a months notice to be given for this request

Pony Club

Own your own pony with two hourly lessons a day, grooming, feeding and stable management all included!

Pony Club Days

Every Saturday, Sunday and school holidays you are able to come in the morning and stay all day. Throughout the day you will receive two one hour instructed lessons which include a fifteen minute walk along the bridle ways. You will also learn how to muck out, tack up, and the general care of your pony/horse. During these days we have a number of staff who will supervise children at all times to ensure their safety.

Pony Club Weeks

Would you like to own your own pony or horse for a week? Whilst children are off school, we provide a course from Tuesday to Sunday which allows riders to experience how to look after a pony or horse. In this week you will recieve two one hour lessons a day, and will be able to keep the pony of your choice in a stable for the week, it will be your job to take care of the pony by mucking out everyday, feeding and grooming. You will also learn how to tack up and untack, along with grooming. During these weeks, we have a number of staff who will supervise the children at all times to ensure their safety.

Please note: when riding, we encourage you to wear a flat pair of boots prefably with a small heel, long trousers and a jacket. We can supply riding hats and boots if needed, and have some childrens body protectors if requested.
